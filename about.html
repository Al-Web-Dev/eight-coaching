About Us

eight Leadership Coaching is a London based coaching and leadership consultancy working with clients across the country. We deliver strengths-based coaching, tailored and designed to get the best possible outcomes for our clients.

We work with you to motivate you, provide focus and support you to plan the next steps of your professional journey. Together we will ensure you understand your strengths to elevate you to the next level whether that is part of a leadership team or as an entrepreneur.

We are here to liberate you from your inner voice and self-doubt, build your confidence and enable you to take that leap to the next phase of your career.

We have worked with clients across a range of industries including finance, media and healthcare. Find out what our clients say about working with us.

Meet our founder Lisa Anderton

Lisa Anderton is a highly skilled executive coach who thrives on seeing others reach their potential and achieve goals they never thought were possible.

Her entire career has focused on building relationships, making connections and collaborating with individuals and organisations.

Lisa says “I founded eight Leadership Coaching because I am passionate about supporting others to unlock their potential.

I am constantly amazed at how much desire, drive and determination people have within themselves and I love it when I am working with clients and we get ‘that’ moment. ‘That’ moment is the point where things click for someone and they realise they can achieve their goals and be successful. I can physically see the realisation pass across a client’s face and it truly brings me joy.

"At eight Leadership Coaching it is our job to help people understand themselves better – their values, what drives them, their strengths – all with the aim of ensuring that people are working towards their most rewarding and fulfilling professional lives.”
