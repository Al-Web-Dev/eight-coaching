Strength Based Leadership Designed to Liberate, Elevate and Motivate

Do you want to take the next step in your career but need to find your focus?

At eight Leadership Coaching we provide strengths-based leadership coaching to help you unlock your true career potential. We know your next step is within you and we work with you to find the clarity and drive to take that step, no matter how big or small.

As well as working with individuals on their leadership journeys, we also work with businesses to improve organisational leadership, employee engagement and productivity.

“At eight Leadership Coaching it is our job to help you understand yourself better - your values, your drivers, your strengths - all with the aim of ensuring that you are working towards your most rewarding and fulfilling Professional life.”

Lisa Anderson, Founder, eight Leadership Coaching
